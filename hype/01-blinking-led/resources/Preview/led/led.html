Circuit 1 - Blinking an LED
?
Red LED
330 Ohm Resistor
For this circuit you will need a 330Ω Resistor and a Red LED.
Insert your MicroView and Programmer with Pin 1 in hole C9.
Select your 330Ω resistor and insert it into points B12 and B17 of your breadboard.
Select your Red LED and insert it into points A16 and A17 of your breadboard.
Start Circuit 2
